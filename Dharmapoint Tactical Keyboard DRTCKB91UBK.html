Introduction

The Dharmapoint Tactical Keyboard DRTCKB91UBK is a 91 key JIS layout keyboard made by Topre for Dharmapoint (typeset DHARMAPOINT or DHARMA POINT), a Japanese computer gaming peripheral company. It is the only Topre keyboard that Dharmapoint offered and it is no longer in production.

Description

The DRTCKB91UBK is essentially a rebranded 91UBK Realforce keyboard with some cosmetic and functional modifications that set it apart from a normal Realforce keyboard. A 'DHARMAPOINT' logo occupies the nameplate section in the top-left corner where the 'REΛLFORCE' logo would normally appear. The keycaps are laser etched and the legends are in a much larger font size than any Realforce keyboard. It is also one of the only Topre keyboards available in the tenkeyless form factor that has 30 g uniform weighted keys, and in fact the only 30 g board available in 91-key JIS layout. Key-stoppers and an operational manual are also supplied with the keyboard.

Uniform weight issue

The keyboard attracted some criticism from customers about its advertised 30 g uniform weighting. Some users felt that the switches on the DRTCKB91UBK were noticeably heavier than 30 g[1][2]. There is very likely some truth to these claims, because when compared side by side with the Realforce 89S (which is also a 30 g uniform keyboard), the required force to actuate is noticeably higher on the DRTCKB91UBK. The negative feedback from users was compelling enough for Dharmapoint to issue a statement on its product page[3].
